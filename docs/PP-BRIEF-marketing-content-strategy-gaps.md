## Context

ParallaxEdge has two approved content strategy documents:
- `youtube-content-strategy.md` v1.0 (April 7, 2026)
- `tiktok-content-strategy.md` v1.0 (April 7, 2026)

Both are well-structured. This brief requests targeted updates to address four gaps identified on April 12, 2026.

## Gap 1 — Calendar Shift (Both Platforms)

Both strategies designate April 7 as Phase 1 start. The actual start date is April 12 — a 5-day slip. The Phase 1 calendar (through April 30) must be revised to reflect April 12 as Day 1 of production. Required output: updated Phase 1 content calendars for both platforms, compressed to fit April 12–30 without dropping any video topics. Indicate which videos can be combined, trimmed, or pushed to Phase 2 if compression isn't feasible. Preserve Phase 2 and Phase 3 calendars unchanged unless strictly necessary.

## Gap 2 — TikTok Promo CTA Consistency

The YouTube strategy includes explicit promo cohort CTAs: "First 1,000 get Sharp tier free through July 2" and "Last chance: free Sharp tier ends July 2." The TikTok strategy has no equivalent CTA. Required output: add promo cohort CTA language to TikTok Phase 2 (late May) and Phase 3 content calendar. Provide 2–3 TikTok-native script hooks for promo CTA videos (30–45 seconds, standalone, conversational tone).

## Gap 3 — Video Production Toolchain Recommendation

Neither strategy specifies a video production toolchain. The platform is operated by a sole founder on Fedora Linux. Required output: recommend a minimal Fedora-compatible video production stack for screen recording, short-form vertical video editing (TikTok 9:16, YouTube Shorts), on-screen text/caption overlays matching brand typography (IBM Plex Mono, DM Sans), and thumbnail creation. Constraints: open source or low-cost, native Fedora Linux, no Adobe suite. Provide specific tool names, install method, and recommended workflow.

## Gap 4 — Video Agent Team Coordination

The Protean Pursuits Video Agent team is available. Neither content strategy references its role. Required output: define the Video Agent team's role in the content production workflow — which Phase 1 videos to route to the Video Agent team vs. produce directly, what brief format the Video Agent team requires, and suggested handoff cadence for the April 12–30 window.

## Deliverable Format

Return all outputs as updates to existing strategy documents (version bump to v1.1) plus a standalone production toolchain recommendation. All documents in Markdown.
